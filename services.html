Contact person in Estonia

The law requires that the contact person in Estonia must be a law office, an audit bureau, a notary office or a company licensed to provide such services. If the
data is not entered into the Estonian business register, the company will be removed from the register within 3 months.

We have the necessary license and we offer the service of adding the necessary contact person to the company, as well as changing the legal address.

Our price for contact person service in Estonia is 250 eur for 1 year. As a part of this service we accept and handle all your company's letters and packages.

Bookkeeping

The accounting service includes operative accounting and VAT calculation, preparation of balance sheet and income statements and submitting of tax declarations to the Tax Board. In accordance with the customer’s wishes, we will also calculate employees’ salaries, keep accounts on income tax and social tax.

We submit statistical reporting to Statistics Estonia and the customer pursuant to the valid procedure and deadlines. We also offer the possibility of reorganising and restoring the accounting records of previous periods.

Tax advisory services

The tax advisory services in specific tax issues as well as for everyday business operations are available to both local and international customers.

We provide advice in income tax, value-added-tax and other tax issues to customers in both the public and the private sector. We give an opinion on the tax-specific consequences of your transactions within the legal framework of Estonia as well as other countries.

We aim at helping the customer to achieve his or her growth potential. Our professional tax advisory services allow the customer to focus on the core activities.

Translated, certified and/or apostilled corporate documents in English delivered to almost any point in the world

Estonia is a digital country where Commercial Registry has strong legal force. That means that you don’t need to have papers when doing business in Estonia – in absolutely most cases it is enough with your personal ID. However, if you plan to act abroad, some documents might be needed. In case you need translated, certified and/or apostilled corporate documents in English delivered to any point in the world, please we are ready to help.
